# Disclaimers

## Required UI disclaimer

Display this or substantially similar wording in the app:

> Parcel boundaries and property data are approximate and provided for general reference only. They are not a legal survey, title opinion, zoning determination, tax advice, or substitute for county/municipal verification.

## Realtor-facing interpretation

The app can help organize public GIS context. It should not present itself as authoritative for:

- Boundary lines
- Encroachments
- Easements
- Legal descriptions
- Zoning compliance
- Permitted uses
- Buildability
- Wetlands/flood determinations
- Title ownership guarantees
- Tax/legal advice

## Suggested parcel detail footer

> Verify all property information with the county, township/municipality, title company, surveyor, or other appropriate professional before relying on it for a transaction.
